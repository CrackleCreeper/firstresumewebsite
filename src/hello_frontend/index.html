Major AI Models

Explore some of the most influential AI models in recent years.

1. GPT-4

Developed by OpenAI, GPT-4 is a state-of-the-art language model that can understand and generate human-like text.

2. BERT

Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT) by Google is designed for understanding the context of words in search queries.

3. DALL-E

DALL-E, also from OpenAI, generates images from textual descriptions, showcasing the power of generative models.

4. ResNet

ResNet (Residual Networks) is a convolutional neural network architecture that enables training of very deep networks, used widely in image classification tasks.

5. Transformer

The Transformer model, introduced by Vaswani et al., revolutionized natural language processing by using attention mechanisms for better context understanding.

6. T5

The Text-To-Text Transfer Transformer (T5) is designed to convert all NLP tasks into a text-to-text format, making it highly versatile.
